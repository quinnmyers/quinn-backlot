Person 1 | Dana Richie

CEO | Executive Producer & Executive Creative Director

Award-winning journalist and seasoned story-pro Dana Richie is Executive Producer and CEO of Backlot Productions. Since forming the company in 2003, Dana has been the company’s creative director and showrunner. Under Dana’s leadership, she and her team has created both original and partnered content that has been embraced by studios, networks, record labels and top US brands. Under Dana’s vision, Backlot has experienced tremendous growth from entertainment marketing production company to branded video content agency. Client assignments she is responsible for include global marketing TV and film content for Sony Pictures Television; entertainment digital spots and series for VH1, Fuse, Crackle and Yahoo!; lifestyle series development for Hulu, plus branded integration and video partnerships with Heineken, Scope, Cover Girl, Starburst, Vitamin Water, Tecate, Burger King, Hard Rock Cafe and GM to name a few. Dana’s the creative force behind every Backlot story. Having enjoyed a 20+ career in Hollywood, she has previously worked at ABC News 20/20 where she worked with Barbara Walters in NY and won several awards including a Cine Golden Eagle and Genesis Award. She’s also worked at CNN, and VH1 where she ran News, Series and Specials, becoming the company’s top celebrity interviewer and director, marketing A-list film, music and TV projects. Along with the company’s entertainment and branded projects, Dana takes pride in Backlot’s non-profit endeavors which include videos with the American Heart Association, Lupus LA, Cancer Schmancer, Point Foundation, Friends Of The Family, B’nai B’rith, Save The Music and The Concern Foundation; she also volunteers for Stand Up To Cancer.
